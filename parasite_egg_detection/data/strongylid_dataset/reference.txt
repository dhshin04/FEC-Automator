general_images/ has images of general strongylid eggs (ones that are not necessarily hookworms)

images/ has random set of images used by DataLoaders (often combination of general images, hookworms
    images, and/or images of non-strongylid eggs)

saved_images/ has set of random images used during training the model

xml_annotations/ has annotations for general strongylid eggs (bounding boxes and associated image name)

labels.json has annotations for all images in images folder

saved_labels.json has annotations used during training the model
